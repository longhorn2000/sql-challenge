

Question #3 List the manager of each department with the following information: department number, department name, the manager's employee number, last name, first name.

SELECT
m.dept_no,
dept_name,
m.emp_no,
first_name,
last_name,
m.from_date,
m.to_date
FROM dept_manager m
LEFT JOIN departments on m.dept_no = departments.dept_no
LEFT JOIN employee on m.emp_no = employee.emp_no;
